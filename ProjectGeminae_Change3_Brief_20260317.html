1. Change Summary

Change 3 (ref. Feb 18 sponsor meeting) fixes a unit inconsistency in the data preparation step. Previously, injection volume W was log-transformed only inside the predictive hold-out function, and pressure P was never transformed. The causal estimator and the predictive model were therefore operating in different units. The fix applies np.log1p() to both W and P upstream in process_file() (dowhy_ci.py, after column rename), so all downstream analysis uses consistently transformed inputs. Negative P values are clipped to zero before the transform (idle-well interpretation). All replaced code is retained in-file with --- CHANGE 3 --- markers.
